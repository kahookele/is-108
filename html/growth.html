Self-growth in the context of my personal strengths and gifts?
  1. Educational Experience - I took a break from school when I felt lost and uncertain, using that time to explore different jobs and passions, which ultimately helped me discover what I truly wanted. Returning to school with a renewed sense of purpose, I now approach my goals with clarity and determination to learn about technology today and how we connect the world with something as simple as a phone.
  2. Roots and Routes - My roots and routes are a gift, shaping my character with every new experience, connection, and challenge I face. The greatest gift I've received from this journey is a renewed sense of self and a better version of me.
  3. Ho'opono Communication - Demonstrating grace during arguments is a crucial trait for effective communication. I've learned that conflicts can be resolved peacefully without resorting to physical means.
  4. Working Styles - I thoroughly enjoyed gaining a deeper understanding of my working style. Being an Executor is definitely a strength, but I'm also aware of the challenges that come with it.
  5. Mindfulness - I've learned that practicing mindfulness can significantly enhance self-improvement, help manage stress effectively, and contribute to a healthier lifestyle.
