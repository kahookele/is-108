Personal responsibilities and and contributions?
Taking personal responsibility for the land and people of Hawaii is deeply connected to the values and insights I have gained through my educational experience. During my time exploring different paths, I discovered the significance of learning with intention and purpose. As I now focus on understanding how technology connects us, I also recognize the importance of how it can be used as a benefit in learning. This balance allows me to use my education not just for personal growth but to contribute meaningfully to the community by fostering connections that honor and uplift Hawaiian culture and its people.

My roots and routes have played a crucial role in shaping who I am today. Each experience and challenge I've faced along the way has deepened my connection to the land and people of Hawaii, instilling in me a sense of responsibility to give back to my friends, family, and myself. This journey has gifted me a renewed sense of self, which now drives me to contribute to the preservation and celebration of Hawaiian traditions. By integrating modern advancements with a deep respect for these traditions, I aim to ensure that they continue to thrive in harmony with the changing world.

In addition, my focus on ho'opono connection and understanding my working style has further strengthened my ability to contribute positively to the Hawaiian community. Practicing mindfulness and demonstrating grace during conflicts are essential to building and maintaining healthy relationships within the community. By being aware of my working style's strengths and challenges, I can approach my contributions to Hawaii in a way that is thoughtful, respectful, and aligned with the values of aloha. This ensures that my efforts support the well-being of both the land and its people, fostering a future where tradition and progress coexist harmoniously.
