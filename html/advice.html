My 5 pieces of advice for you all?
Be Open-Minded

"College can be intimidating for many, but as a returning student, I can assure you that everyone’s journey is unique. This class is an inclusive, judgment-free zone where you are encouraged to express yourself in any way that feels right for you."

Be Resourceful

"As you journey through college life, you'll encounter many obstacles, including challenges related to your personal life choices, college life, and even your mental health. The Lunalilo Scholars teachers and peer mentors are here to help you in any way they can. Please, ask them for help whenever needed."

Take Care of Yourself

"Unhealthy sleep can lead to bad study habits. Put yourself first. Take care of your mental health or anything you consider a neccessity." "What mental health needs is more sunlight, more candor, and more unashamed conversation." — Glenn Close.

Know What You Want

"This was the main reason I dropped out the first time. Know your dreams and align your goals with them. For those who are still deciding on a career, you can take the Myers-Briggs test to see which career suits you. It's the biggest reason I came back to school."

Have Fun

"You're in college now! Don't be too stressed. You get to make your own decisions, including building relationships with others. Go out and have fun with the people you meet. Enjoy your youthful years, but just don't do anything you might regret later"
